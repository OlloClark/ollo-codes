About Me

Hi! I'm Ollo Clark, a software engineer with a background in theater, education, and management consultancy. A fascination with language, logic, and puzzles allows me to tackle difficult or opaque problems with curiosity, energy and humor. Six years of stage work taught me real teamwork, while eight years of teaching taught me communication, connection, and patience.

I recently graduate from General Assembly's Software Engineering Intensive. Below, you can find several projects, each built in no more than five days. Enjoy!
